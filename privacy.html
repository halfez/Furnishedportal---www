Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 20, 2026  ·  Questions: support@furnishedportal.com

FurnishedPortal ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website at furnishedportal.com and the software platform we provide to independent midterm rental hosts. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information we collect

We collect information you give us directly:

  • Account and onboarding data — name, email address, property details, amenities, photos, and branding information you provide through our intake questionnaire.
  • Payment information — billing details processed by Stripe. We never store your full card number; Stripe handles payment security on our behalf.
  • Communications — messages you send to support@furnishedportal.com or through the platform.

We also collect limited technical data automatically:

  • Pages visited and time spent (via Vercel Analytics, if enabled)
  • Browser type and device, for diagnosing technical issues

How we use your information

  • To set up and operate your FurnishedPortal website and tenant portal
  • To process payments and send receipts
  • To send you service emails (onboarding, billing, product updates)
  • To respond to support requests
  • To improve the platform based on usage patterns

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising.

Your tenants' data

When your tenants submit applications, sign leases, or pay rent through your FurnishedPortal site, their data is collected on your behalf. You are the data controller for your tenants' information. We process it only to provide the platform service to you.

Third-party services we use

  • Stripe — payment processing (stripe.com/privacy)
  • Vercel — hosting and infrastructure (vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy)
  • Resend — transactional email delivery
  • Tally — intake questionnaire form

Data retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active, plus a reasonable period afterward in case of billing disputes or legal obligations. You may request deletion at any time by emailing support@furnishedportal.com.

Your rights

You may request to access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time. Email us at support@furnishedportal.com and we'll respond within 5 business days.

Cookies

We use only functional cookies necessary to operate the platform (session authentication). We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we'll notify active customers by email at least 14 days before the change takes effect.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email support@furnishedportal.com. We're a small team and will respond personally.
